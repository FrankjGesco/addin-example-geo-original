Proximity

This Add-In allows looking up an address to visualize when your fleet was nearby that location in the past.

Find out when team members visited an address by typing it in below. Filter your results by selecting specific vehicles or narrowing the time frame shown.

This add-in will display a maximum of 1000 vehicles at a time.
